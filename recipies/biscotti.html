Biscotti Recipie for Trolls

Brenda's Lasangna Recipe

  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 oz pistacios
  • 1/2 cup of Coca powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 2 tsp nutmeg
  1. combine dry and wet ingredients in a large bowl
  2. once combined form oval shape 2 inches thick to place on partment lined baking sheet
  3. place in preheated 375 degree oven for 120-25 minutes or until cooked
  4. remove from oven and let cool for 30 minutes
  5. cut into buscotti strips and place back in oven for 12 minutes until desired level of doneness, if too soft can place back in oven further
  6. Enjoy
